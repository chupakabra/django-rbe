Location

This page allows you to see the location of other people advocating for a Resource Based Economy. You are free to share your location if you want to and also can see the profiles of users close to you. This allows better activism and coordination and you might discover people which you didn't know before living closer to you than you think.

Skills & Abilities

Being part of the network allows you to enter skills, abilities and interests you have and in which fields you want to advance yourself. You can find people with similar skills to deepen your knowledge or get help for particular problems. I am sure that some people are also willing provide education on new topics and share their knowledge.

Connecting

Networking is one of the most important parts in activism and in spreading ideas in general. It is a network that is based on an invite-approach for the simple reason that it does not require a central authority to allow users in but also makes sure that not everyone can just join if they are not familiar with the presented topic and way of thinking. Once joined you are free to invite people yourself.

Plugin

If you are a software developer, then this page can be used as an OpenId provider which allows you to build additional functionality around it. Use the location data to inform about activism in the region, bring people together on projects with certain skills; the possibilities are endless.
